D4rK QR & Bar Code Scanner is an ad-free, open-source scanner app. It uses the ZXing scanning library.
Is compatible with Material-You design on Android 12+ devices with changeable themes in settings.

You can customize you app experience, like preferred camera or preferred theme using app settings.
